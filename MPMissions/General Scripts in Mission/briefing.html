Shift-Alt-Click Rules

Traditional CRCTI uses "Shift-Alt-Click to open waypoint dialog. In latest TZK this map click will activate different dialogs determined by player's current RTS status.

- Click with AI selected

The "RTS" order dialog will be activated. Since an area is also drawn, pressing enter can rapidly switch to "AREA" dialog.

- Click without AI selected but rectangle drawn

The "AREA" dialog will be activated.

- Click without neither AI selected nor drawn rectangle

The "POINT" dialog or "WAYPOINT" dialog will be activated, depends on which kind is activated in last time. Pressing enter can rapidly switch to another one.

Turn ON/OFF drawing RTS

Traditional CRCTI uses "Alt-Click without AI selected" to set player group waypoints rapidly. In latest TZK Alt-Click is also applied to draw RTS markers. Activate radio channel 0-0-1 to switch between these 2 status.
